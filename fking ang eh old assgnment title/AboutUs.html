ABOUT US

Eclipse Studio was founded in 2016 to help game companies in developing, maintaining and supporting their games. Focused around gameplay development, we are committed to creating an immersive gameplay experience to players across the globe. We have helped develop many popular titles such as Boblox & amonkass. Contributing our skills and knowledge into the world of gaming to make boundary breaking content for all. Eclipse Studio is currently located in Malaysia with plans to expand across Asia as well as other places.
